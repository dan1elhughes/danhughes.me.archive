Live at PlugnPlay
Live performance PlugnPlay Studios
For this performance, I used my entire collection of drum machines, samplers, synthesizers and more to create a live electronic act.
The performance featured vocals from many Reading-based artists, including the always incredible <a href="https://www.facebook.com/CamilleJobinMusic">Camille Jobin</a>.
A video of one of the songs I did with Shaquille Howe and Nathan Cross can be found <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=k4UM4bMDiIw">here</a>.
